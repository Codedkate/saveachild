About Us

We are non-profit organization with the primary goal to create a better way to deal with hunger and reduction of food wastages.
Our mission is to alleviate hunger through the use of donated food and other resources. We will never give up until our society is free from hunger.

What We Do

We advocate for an operation save the food save a child organization where we collect unused/used materials that are distributed to the less privileged in our society. In a nutshell we fight extreme hunger.

Food Bank

We connect the world of wastage to world of needy by rescuing edible surplus food from stakeholders with the food supply chain e.g Wholesellers, Manufactures, Retailers, Supermarkets, Resturants, Hotels, Market women and supply to vulnerable beneficiaries. e.g children's home, vulnerable communities.

Skills Acqusition

We collaborate with other organizations and artisan to help the less privillaged gain skills that will make them usefull to the society as well as being able to meet their daily needs

Education

Just like Nelson Manndela would say'Education is the most powerful weapon against poverty' with our partners we help educate the less privileged and via these very program, we have received numerous testimonies and more are still coming in.

Why choose us?

We deal with first hand information which means we go down to where these people live, interact with them and understand thier needs and with my ever ready to work team we never leave a home until there is a smile on thier faces.

We offer timely services

My team and I are always on time and ever ready to put a smile on every child's face.

There is never a place too far to reach

Distance is never a problem with us for as long as there is a soul who is in need, we are always there...
